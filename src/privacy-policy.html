Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Your health information is personal. We take that seriously. This policy explains clearly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Important Notice: Beyond the Hairline is a personal self-tracking tool and is not a covered entity under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). This app is not affiliated with any healthcare provider, insurance company, or medical institution. Do not store information here that you would not be comfortable sharing outside of a clinical setting. For protected medical records, please use your healthcare provider's official patient portal.

1. Who We Are

Beyond the Hairline is a personal health tracking application designed to help individuals living with scarring alopecia — including Central Centrifugal Cicatricial Alopecia (CCCA) and related conditions — track symptoms, document their journey, and prepare for medical appointments.

2. What Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Beyond the Hairline, we may collect the following:

  • Your email address (used for account creation and login)
  • Health symptom logs you enter manually (such as pain levels, itching, shedding)
  • Photos you choose to upload to document your hair and scalp
  • Doctor visit notes, questions, and appointment records you create
  • Treatment and diet information you choose to track

We do not collect your name, phone number, address, or payment information unless you voluntarily provide it.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide the features of this app to you. Specifically:

  • To display your personal health tracking data back to you
  • To allow you to document and review your health journey over time
  • To send account-related emails (such as email confirmation and password reset)

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your health information for research or marketing without your explicit consent.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database platform with industry-standard encryption. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Access to your personal data is protected by the password you create.

We strongly encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to keep your login credentials private.

5. Your Health Data Is Sensitive

We recognize that information about your hair loss condition, symptoms, and medical visits is sensitive personal health information. We treat it with the highest level of care. Only you can access your personal health records within this app.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data you have entered into the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Correct any inaccurate information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Beyond the Hairline is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
